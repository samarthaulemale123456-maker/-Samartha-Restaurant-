Tradition meets

Located in the heart of Lakhala, Washim, Shree Samartha Restaurant is a premier destination for families seeking an extraordinary pure vegetarian dining experience.

We believe in honoring traditional Indian culinary arts while providing a modern, comfortable, and highly hospitable environment. Every dish is a testament to our dedication to quality, prepared with the freshest ingredients and authentic spices.
